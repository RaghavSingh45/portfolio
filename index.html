Raghav Singh

Raghav is a Ph.D. candidate at the MSS department at SUNY Buffalo, where he also received the MS in MIS degree in 2020. He continues to author research papers in various journals and conferences, with his current research focusing on cybersecurity leadership. Raghav's other research interests include cybersecurity, healthcare IT, data privacy, and AI. He has reviewed for various research publication avenues such as AMCIS, ICIS, and HICSS. Besides scholarly engagements, Raghav has four years of experience in Product and Project Management between 2017 and 2022. He also served in the Indian Army between 2013 to 2015.
